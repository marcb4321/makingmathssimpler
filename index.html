Basic probability

$$ \text{Probability of event A} = P(A) = \frac {\text{Number of outcomes with A}} {\text{Total Number of outcomes}} $$







Result:

The Addition Rule

$$ P(A\ \text{or}\ B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A\ \text{and}\ B) $$









Result:

The Multiplication Rule

$$ P(A\ \text{and}\ B) = P(A) * P(B|A)\ \text{or}\ P(B) * P(A|B) $$

The Complement Rule

$$ P(\text{not}\ A) = 1 - P(A) $$

Bayes' Rule

$$ P(A|B) = \frac{ P(B|A) P(A)}{ P(B) } $$
